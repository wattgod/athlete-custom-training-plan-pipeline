## Marketplace listing

**Title**: Gravel Climbing Plan — 12 Weeks
**Tagline**: For mountain gravel: sustained climbs decide the day.

Built for climbing-heavy gravel races (8,000ft+ over 100 miles). Sustained climbing strength: low-cadence muscular work, threshold repeats, and long rides with real elevation. Includes altitude arrival guidance for high-elevation events.

Built for 6-10 hours/week. Tuesday/Thursday intensity, Saturday long ride,
Monday off. Every workout has power AND heart-rate/RPE targets — no power
meter required. Includes strength track.
