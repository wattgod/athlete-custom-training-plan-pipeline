# BUILD MANIFEST — Fast Gravel Plan (16 weeks)

**SKU id**: `gravel-fast-punchy-16wk` &nbsp; **Workouts**: 94

## Marketplace listing

**Title**: Fast Gravel Plan — 16 Weeks
**Tagline**: For short, violent gravel: speed and surges.

Built for shorter, fast gravel races (40-75 miles) decided by repeated hard surges and group dynamics. VO2 development is the spine; race simulations rehearse attacking and recovering at race pace. Handling skills under fatigue included.

Built for 6-10 hours/week. Tuesday/Thursday intensity, Saturday long ride,
Monday off. Every workout has power AND heart-rate/RPE targets — no power
meter required. Includes strength track.
