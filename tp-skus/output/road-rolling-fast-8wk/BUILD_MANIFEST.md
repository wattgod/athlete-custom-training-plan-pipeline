## Marketplace listing

**Title**: Fast Road Race Plan — 8 Weeks
**Tagline**: For fast, rolling road races: speed wins, surges decide.

Built for fast rolling road races and fondos decided by group riding and repeated accelerations. VO2 intervals are the spine, threshold supports, and race simulations train surge-and-settle rhythm — the skill of going hard twice.

Built for 6-10 hours/week. Tuesday/Thursday intensity, Saturday long ride,
Monday off. Every workout has power AND heart-rate/RPE targets — no power
meter required. Includes strength track.
