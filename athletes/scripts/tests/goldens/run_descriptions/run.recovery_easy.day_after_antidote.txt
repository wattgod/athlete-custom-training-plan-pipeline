-5min warm-up @ RPE 1-2.

-20min easy run @ RPE 2-3.
-Cadence: Light, quiet steps; walk briefly if form deteriorates.

-5min cool-down @ RPE 1-2.

PROGRESSION:
-Level 3/6: Development - Standard training load, building consistency

NUTRITION:
-None needed at this duration.

HYDRATION:
-Drink to thirst; rehydrate normally after the run.
